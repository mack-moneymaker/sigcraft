Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add this signature to Gmail?

Click "Copy to Clipboard", then go to Gmail ‚Üí Settings (gear icon) ‚Üí See all settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí Signature. Click in the signature box and paste (Ctrl+V / Cmd+V). Click Save Changes.

Does it work with Outlook?

Yes! Copy the signature, then in Outlook go to Settings ‚Üí View all Outlook settings ‚Üí Compose and reply. Paste your signature in the Email Signature box.

Can I use my own photo?

Yes ‚Äî upload a photo or paste a hosted image URL. Using a URL is recommended for better email deliverability since base64 images can be large.

Is SigCraft really free?

Yes! The free tier includes 3 templates, unlimited signatures, and all core features. SigCraft Pro ($5/mo) adds premium templates, removes the small branding badge, and unlocks custom banners and click analytics.

Why does my signature look different in some email clients?

Email clients render HTML differently. SigCraft uses table-based layouts and inline styles for maximum compatibility across Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail, Yahoo, and Thunderbird.

Can I customize colors and fonts?

Yes! Use the Style section to pick your brand colors, text color, font family, and separator style. Changes update in real-time in the preview.

Can I save multiple signatures?

Yes! Use the "Saved Signatures" section to name and save multiple signatures. Your current signature auto-saves as you type. Switch between saved signatures with the dropdown.
